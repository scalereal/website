Hiring Process

How We Hire

Our process is designed to be respectful of your time while helping us both find the right fit.

1

Application

Submit your resume and tell us about your passion and experience.

2

Screening Call

A 20-minute chat with our HR team to understand your expectations.

3

Technical Round

Showcase your architecture and coding skills through a hands-on challenge.

4

Culture Alignment

A conversation about your growth mindset, core values and working style.

5

Offer

Finalizing the details to officially welcome you to our Remote-first team!
