Client Engagement Flow

Our streamlined process ensures a smooth start to every project.

1

NDA & Introduction

We sign an NDA and conduct a quick intro call to understand your project requirements.

2

Profile Sharing

We share engineer profiles with expertise, experience, and rate details for your review.

3

Interviews or Trial

Interview selected engineers or start a 2-week trial, billed only if you continue.

4

Proposal & SOW

We prepare a Statement of Work with timelines, pricing, and deliverables.

5

Kickoff

Setup tools, conduct kickoff meeting, and start Sprint 0 within 1 week.
