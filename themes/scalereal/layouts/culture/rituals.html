Team Rituals

How We Connect

Beyond projects and deadlines, our rituals help us connect as people, celebrate progress, and keep the team spirit grounded and genuine.

Demo Fridays

Every Friday, teams showcase what they've built—fostering cross-pollination and celebrating progress.

Hackathons

Quarterly hackathons where anyone can pitch an idea and build a prototype in 48 hours.

Open Mic Sessions

Monthly sessions where anyone can present on any topic—tech, travel, cooking, or philosophy.

Mentorship Circles

Senior engineers mentor juniors through structured 1-on-1 programs that accelerate growth.
